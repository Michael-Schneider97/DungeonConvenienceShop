Features

Player shop proceeds through levels
Economy
Player can purchase various types of items
Player can purchase permits
Customers come and buy goods
Employees can be hired and assigned to tasks
Multipe income streams


#Player shop proceeds through levels:

Level 0: Rug on the side of a road. 
Level 1: Kiosk / Stall
Level 2-5: Physical Store- requires permits and upfront cost
Level 6+: Additional locations or installation of departments/wings.

#Economy

Currency system uses levels of metal
E.g. copper, silver, gold, platinum/gem
Each is a multiple of the last

Item values increase semi exponentially with level
E.g. Lesser healing->standard healing->ultimate healing potions
might be 1 gold then 10 gold then 100 gold

Permits and upgrades have relatively high upfront costs
but increase income potential

#Player can purchase multiple types of items

Permits are a single time purchase item that either
unlocks features or enables upgrades.
